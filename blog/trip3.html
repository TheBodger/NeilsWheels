Malvern, Ledbury and Hills

May 2025, Walking in the Malvern hills with the sun shining, the RHS show and a night with the Pink Floyd experience - this is my favourite place so far !

The camp site looked across the valleys and hills that the River Wye and tributaries have carved over eons. Relaxing in itself, The whole trip was one bonus after another. Planned around a birthday present to see the Pink Floyd experience (again), we had no idea what to expect. Ledbury's historical medieval town, Great Malvern's views across its majestic hills, the Hill walks up steep wooded valleys onto rolling grasslands and the unexpected RHS show all meant that we fell in love with this area.
